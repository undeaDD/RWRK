About us

RWRK is a whitelabelled upcycling as a service platform, aiming to help luxury fashion brands extend the life cycle of garments, and also help brands engage with new audiences through unique brand garments.

Our key USP is our rare expertise in upcycling, and running an operationally and logistically challenging business which has never before been done at scale. Using our patterns, blocks and design ‘IP’ we are able to create upcycled garments at scale and Our in-house design capabilities ensure high quality, and continued professional development in the field of upcycling. We work closely with brands to ensure that designs are aligned with their brand image. With a portfolio of RWRK designers available, brands can select to work with designers whose styles best match their brand image.
