Frequently asked questions

Upcycling is a form of recycling that reworks unwanted garments into a better-quality and higher value product.

  • Saving materials from landfill
  • Reducing what goes into landfill
  • Minimal use of natural resources
  • Doing your bit for the environment
  • Creating unique garments

We primarily work with eco-conscious brands who have a stockpile of unwanted garments.

Our materials are sourced from the brands we work with. Our aim is to keep materials in the production and consumption loop and away from landfill and incinerators.

We upcycle using most materials, for example; cotton, polyester, leather, silk, rubber and more. Feel free to reach out to us here, if you have any questions regarding this.

It can take up to 3 weeks to produce the first sample. 6 weeks to manufacture depending on the volume, complexity and any particularities that may require outsourcing to specialists.
