Social Impact

Purchasing an upcycled jacket saves as much water as you'd use to fill over 1,200 standard water bottles and offsets the carbon emissions produced by driving a car for approximately 20 miles.

Join our efforts to close the loop on fashion waste. Return your old dustbags to us and receive incentives or discounts on your new purchases. We ensure that your old items are recycled or upcycled responsibly.

Care

Caring for your clothes is caring for the environment.

To keep your jackets and coats clean, you only need to freshen them up and go over them with a cloth or a clothes brush. This process is more gentle on fabrics and also reduces water and energy consumption when washing.

Do not wash

Do not use bleach

Do not iron

Dry clean with tetracholoroethylene

Do not tumble dry
